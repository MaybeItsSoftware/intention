Say goodbye to rigid app blockers and easily bypassed screen time limits. Intention is a smart, AI-powered focus gate that places a supportive AI coach between you and the apps that pull you away from your real goals.

Instead of a hard block or a simple timer, opening a distracting app begins a brief, mindful conversation. Your coach asks what you are trying to do, prompts you to reflect, and decides whether to grant you access based on your actual goals.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Choose your Apps: Add distracting social media, games, or feed-based apps to your blocklist.
2. Mindful Interception: When you open a blocked app, Intention temporarily pauses it and opens a coaching chat.
3. State Your Intention: Explain what you need to do (e.g., "Check directions for my meeting" or "Reply to an urgent message").
4. Earn Your Minutes: If you have a specific, time-bounded purpose, your coach grants you a few minutes of access. If it's aimless scrolling, the coach gently reminds you of your goals and suggests better alternatives.
5. No Easy Way Out: You can only update your goals and coach instructions by talking to the coach. No simple text settings toggle to bypass the rules in a weak moment!

KEY FEATURES
• Bring Your Own Key (BYOK): Set up your own API key to connect to leading AI models: Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (GPT), Google (Gemini), or Groq. You pay only for what you use, directly to the provider.
• Smart Access Gatekeeper: The AI coach uses secure tool-calls to grant access and update your profile, ensuring a structured, spoof-proof flow.
• Goal-Oriented Profile: Your coach builds a profile of who you are, what you're working on, and what habits you want to break. This profile is shaped and updated only through your conversations.
• Exponential Skepticism & Daily Cap: Earning extra screen time gets progressively harder throughout the day. Once you hit your daily grant limit, the coach offers motivational support, but no more screen time.
• Mindful & Non-Judgmental: The coach is trained to be supportive, curious, and warm—never lecturing, but always helping you build self-awareness.
• 100% Private: Your API keys and conversation histories are stored locally on your device. Only direct requests to your chosen AI provider are sent over the network.

ACCESSIBILITY SERVICE DISCLOSURE
Intention uses Android's Accessibility Service API. This permission is required to detect when you launch apps on your blocklist and to overlay the AI coaching interface. 
• Intention only monitors the package name of the foreground app to check if it is blocked.
• It does not read, capture, or transmit any on-screen text, keyboard inputs, or personal data.
• You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.
